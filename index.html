world vision evangelism church of phoenix

At world vision evangelism church of phoenix we believe in only one God and also his one mediator between God and Men, jesus christ, who gave himself as a ransom for all. we accept the trinity of god the father, the son (jesus christ), and the holy spirit. -1 tim.2:5-6; matt.28:19

 

Our vision is to see that the gospel of jesus christ is preached to all peoples by training future leaders who could be active in our faith through outreach and the creation of a discipleship of worshipers who are willing to worship god in spirit and in truth. we work to help believers to receive spiritual gifts and discover their true calling.

 

World vision Evangelism Chucrh (WVEC) is a Christian religious institution which had its origins from the Evangelical Mission of Damans Church also known as Mission Evangelique Damans in the Democractic Republic of Congo in central Africa. It is founded on the application of the great comission from the Gospel of Matthew, Chapter 28 -Verse 18-20, which states: "And Jesus came and spoke Unto them, saying, "All power is Given unto Me in Heaven and on Earth: Go ye Therefore and Teach All nations; Baptizing them in The name of the Father And of the Son and of the Holy ghost. Teaching them to observe all thing whatsoever I bhave commended you - and low I'm with you always even unto the end of the world -Amen". Read more
